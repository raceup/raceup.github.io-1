The team

We are 70 students from the University of Padova, chosen through a careful and targeted recruiting. Our team is based on values ​​such as collaboration, involvement, willingness to share and learn. Race Up members develop cross-disciplinary skills such as teamwork, project planning and problem solving; skills that will prove to be fundamental in the job market . We believe that only with this environment we can achieve the goals we set for each season. Our commitment continues beyond competitions through a constant research that leads to innovation in materials and working techniques. The desire to innovate pushed the team to introduce, in addition to the internal combustion engine, a second wholly electric car.
